About Me

Xara Shaw: Game Developer, Animator, Writer, Transgender, Odd Person

Hi, my name is Xara Neale Shaw, also known as Xaragame. I’m a black transfemme game developer, 3D animator and writer based out of Los Angeles. I’ve been a Roblox developer against my will since 2021 and a Unity Developer Since 2022. In short, my main goal in life, pushing the gay agenda and destroying neoliberal capitalism. In long, I seek to use my perspective in life to create games and animations that I find interesting while also trying to promote unionization in the game development industry and public transit in Los Angeles. I believe that the solution to most of our crises is one where the working class can organize with one another freely. I created this website in 2023 half as a way for me to try and separate myself further from the parasitic, addictive, life ruining, technofeudalist hellscape that is modern social media while staying online. And half as a way to stand out to college admissions officers. So if you're here to learn more about Xara Shaw, you're in the right place. Here you can find my previous work, my rants, my essays, my opinions, what media I'm into, and you can reach out to me for commissions and whatnot. Anyways, welcome to my spot on the internet, have a look around.
